About Me

I moved to Portland Oregon in 2000, and over the last 16 years I have seen this city change so much. As a bartender I had a chance to meet an amazing group of regulars, friends and co-workers. As an ambulance driver I had a chance to really learn about the city and all of its winding roads. As I establish myself in a career as a developer I wonder what roads will be open to me, what kind of new relationships I will form, and how my city will continue to change in the years to come.
